ReVolver Setup Guide

Connect your Volvo to your Pebble watch in 2 steps.

Requirements:
  • A Volvo car with active Volvo Cars connected services
  • A Volvo ID (the account you use in the Volvo Cars app)

Step 1: Log In

1. Open ReVolver settings on your phone (Pebble app → ReVolver → Settings).
2. Tap Log in with Volvo ID. You'll be redirected to Volvo's secure login page.
3. Sign in with your Volvo ID and approve access when prompted.
4. After authorization, tap Return to Pebble to send your credentials to the watch.

Step 2: Enter Your VIN

5. In the ReVolver settings on your phone, enter your VIN and tap Save.

That's it!

Your Pebble will now show live data from your Volvo — fuel level, range, trip stats, and more. You can also lock/unlock, honk, flash lights, and control climatization from your wrist.

Token Refresh

How tokens work:
  • Access tokens expire after ~60 minutes
  • ReVolver refreshes automatically in the background
  • Refresh tokens expire if unused for 7 days
  • After 6 months, you'll need to log in again
If you see "API err 401": Your token has expired. Re-open settings and log in again.

Privacy & Security

  • ReVolver uses OAuth2 with PKCE — your Volvo ID password is never shared with us
  • Token exchange happens on a secure backend — no secrets in the app
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Volvo ID account
